What We Do

Our Services

From concept to completion, we handle every aspect of your project with precision and craftsmanship.

Custom Decks

Timber and composite decks designed to complement your home and lifestyle. Pool decks, entertainment areas, and more — built to last.

Renovations

Full home renovations including bathrooms, alfresco areas, and structural improvements. We transform your space from the ground up.

Timber Work

Specialist timber framing, cladding, pergolas and outdoor structures. We work with premium hardwoods and composites.

Pergolas & Structures

Custom-designed pergolas, carports, garden sheds and outdoor entertainment structures tailored to your property.

Pool Surrounds

Stunning pool deck installations that are safe, durable, and low maintenance. Merbau, spotted gum, composite — we do it all.

Project Management

End-to-end project management so you don't have to worry. We handle trades, materials, and timelines with total transparency.
